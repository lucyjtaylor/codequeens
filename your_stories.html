Your Stories

Have you ever felt unsafe walking around London as a lone woman? So have we, and that's why we've created our Your Stories map. This map shows the locations of incidents of sexual assault, which have been reported by fellow Support Sisters.

It can serve you in a number of ways:

  • It brings together a picture of which areas of London are particularly dangerous for lone women so that you can plan your journeys around this knowledge
  • It can help you to recognise that you share an experience with another woman who was assaulted by the same person, and might give you the courage to report an assault to help stop this from happening again
  • It can serve as a way for you to report an incident of assault if you do not feel ready to go to the police. We will never report an incident for you, but we use the stories submitted to the map to learn about how and where we should campaign. It gives us the tools and knowledge to fight on your behalf.

Guidlines for Submission

Please be aware that many people using this map may have experienced sexual assault, and so may find it difficult to read these reports. To make this accessible to everyone, please do not use graphic language.

Any submission can be made anonymous upon your request, even after uploading. Just e-mail map@supportsisters.org.uk and we will anonymise your submission.


We'll never share your email with anyone else.
